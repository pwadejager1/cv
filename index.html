Playing College Golf in USA

Golf is the most fascinating sport I ever played; I was privileged to play multiple different types of sports at school level - and most of them at provincial and national level. Golf in undoubtedly my passion! I'm busy preparing to play at professional level, this is my ultimate goal. In context of my preparation I'm playing on the Big Easy IGT Challenge Tour in South Africa. The tour is accommodating current professional golfers and amateurs that want to obtain their player cards to play on the Sunshine Tour. Personally I enjoy travelling and I was fortunate to tour to the USA, Dallas-Texas, in 2017 when I participated in an international Rugby 7's tournament. The reason I enjoy travelling so much is because of all the people I get to meet, and the friendships that one fosters due to the opportunity presented. An opportunity to play golf in the USA, at college level, is the ultimate dream! It will not just allow me to meet more players/friends, but also to compete at this very competitive level. This will also allow me the opportunity to study full-time, something that I'm not able to do currently. I would like to think that I work with purpose to reach my life goals, and have considerable focus and perseverance.
